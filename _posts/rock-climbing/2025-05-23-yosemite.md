This spring I got to take my first climbing trip to Yosemite Valley. Over a couple weeks I got to climb many classic routes including Astroman, Royal Arches, Serenity-Sons, Freeblast, and Lurking Fear. Overall, the Valley met my expectations: Starbucks, crowds, and beginner climbers alongside the highest concentration of quality granite rock climbs and crusher rock climbers I've ever seen.
