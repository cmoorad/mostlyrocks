    <h2 id="chamonix"><b>Chamonix</b></h2>
    We headed to cooler temps in Chamonix and were immediately rewarded with stunning views from the Mer du Glace glacier. Walking out of the house with all our gear and taking the bus, to the train, to the gondola, to the glacier, to the via ferrata, to the Envers hut left us feeling giddy. I don't know if the Euros do it <i>right</i> but they do it <i>different</i> ‚Äî and whatever else it is <i>it's very rad</i>.
