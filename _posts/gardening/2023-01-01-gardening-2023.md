This year I was too focused on rock climbing to do much gardening. Staying true to the name of this blog, for me it's mostly about the rocks. That said, it was my first year growing pumpkins and mammoth sunflowers which were cool to grow, and I also did a compost experiment which yielded striking results. I can't remember the bad compost (maybe Cowlitz County Mushroom?), but Cedar Grove is what I'll be using from now on.
