It was a great winter! I stayed local since I was taking care of Jack who was unable to do much post meniscus surgery. I got to take the sled out a few times, ski at WA Pass, in the Teanaway, and at Holden Village! I especially liked skiing around the Tye Peak zone this year for some reason. There's always a different "favorite" zone; this year I was liking the ease of access, diversity in terrain options, lack of crowds (if you know where to go), and pockets of deep powder. I'm most excited to ski in the Teanaway next year since it has all those things but better.
